Ticket to work
Apply for this service Contact us

We designed the Ticket to Work program to help participants pursue a career and achieve financial independence from benefits. Cares of Washington supports participants with individualized, no-cost services and a dedicated Employment Specialist assigned to every client.

Services

  • Job readiness training
  • Resume/cover letter writing
  • Interview skills
  • Application completion
  • Using the internet
  • Job search resources
  • Skills assessment
  • Job placement assistance
  • Resource materials
  • Job accommodation advice
  • Benefits assistance

You're eligible if you are

  • Between ages 18–64
  • Receiving SSI or SSDI benefits
  • Have an available Ticket from SSI/SSDI
