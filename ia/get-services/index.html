Overview

Cares of Washington provides services through the following national programs: Ticket to Work, Developmental Disabilities Division, Foundational Community Support, Division of Vocational Rehabilitation, and Connect for Success.

Once a program is identified and approved, you'll receive personalized employment services to help you find and keep a job. As a CARF-accredited organization, we are committed to delivering measurable, accountable, and highest quality programs through a comprehensive intake, preparation and a 2-day peer review process.
